Studying Art

When I was in highschool I always wanted to be an artist but nobody really believed in me. I ended up going to a law school for four years, but I still realize that my life hasn't satisfied me if I don't try to pursue my dream as an artist one day. After five years graduating from Law School, I finally decide to pursue my art diploma this year in La Trobe College Melbourne.

After I discussed with my psychologist and took a talent test, I went to my parents and showed them the result. That's the turning point where I suddenly got a confidence and bravery to take a leap of faith in changing my career direction.
